Brexit Timeline

Brexit has happened, the UK is now outside the EU. It is now time to see if the promises match reality.

The news cycle is also bery busy, pushing important Brexit stories of the front covers of papers and allowing the government to escape accountability. By creating a timeline of stories we hope to make it easy for the public to see and follow the unfolding problems of Brexit.
